ABOUT

Felix Xu is an illustrator, designer, and storyteller based in Collegeville, PA, who will be graduating with a BFA in Illustration from the Rhode Island School of Design in the summer of 2025. He draws inspiration for his artwork from historical time periods, the fantasy genre, and aquatic environments.

Outside of illustration, Felix’s interests include cephalopods, writing, amateur web design, and Dungeons & Dragons.
