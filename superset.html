CITY, OH CITY

COMICS



SEASONAL FORTUNE DECK

Designs for a deck of playing cards featuring fantastical characters based on the four seasons. Each of the numbered cards represents a different profession (farmer, rogue, woodsman, scribe, knight, apothecary, scholar, magician), and the concept for the deck was that it could be used in a sort of fortune telling game.



SPOT ILLUSTRATIONS



THE CITY OF LIGHT

Environment illustrations for a homebrew D&D setting: a Gilded Age-inspired city set in a world of eternal night.



CITY OF LIGHT COLLECTION

A collection of prop designs based on the aforementioned D&D setting, all based around the concept of various items which utilize magical light.



CITY OF LIGHT 24-HOUR CYCLE

A series of illustrations depicting one location from the aforementioned D&D setting at twelve different times of day.
